Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers

The Department of Chemicals and Petrochemicals was under the Ministry of Industry until December 1989, when it was brought under the Ministry of Petroleum and Chemicals. On June 5, 1991, Department of Chemicals and Petrochemicals was transferred to the Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers.
The department has various divisions under it. The important being:

Chemical Division
Petrochemicals Division
Monitoring and Evaluation Division (M&E Division)

Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya JanAushadhi Pariyojana

Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana was launched by the Department of Pharmaceuticals, in November 2008. Under the scheme, dedicated outlets known as Janaushadhi Kendras are opened to make quality generic medicines available to all at affordable prices.

Problem Statement

The high cost of branded medications remains a significant barrier to healthcare access, leading to financial strain and health disparities. To address this issue, the Janaushadhi portal aims to enhance accessibility and affordability by recommending high-quality generic alternatives to expensive branded drugs. By developing a robust recommendation system that matches branded medicines with their cost-effective generic counterparts based on active pharmaceutical ingredients, and by providing users with a user-friendly interface and educational resources, the portal seeks to increase awareness, improve medication adherence, and ultimately reduce patient expenditures. This initiative will ensure that patients have access to affordable, reliable, and effective treatments, promoting equity in healthcare and alleviating financial burdens.
